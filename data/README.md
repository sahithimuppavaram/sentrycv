# Data

Put two overlapping camera videos here as `cam0.mp4` and `cam1.mp4`.

Fastest options, easiest first:
1. **Webcam + phone.** Record ~2 min of you (and a friend) walking past a
   laptop webcam and a phone aimed at the same area from a different angle.
   Zero download, and it makes a great demo-day story.
2. **WILDTRACK** (multi-camera pedestrians, overlapping views) -
   https://www.epfl.ch/labs/cvlab/data/data-wildtrack/
3. **MOT Challenge** sequences (MOT17 / MOT20) - https://motchallenge.net/
   Single-camera, but perfect for the detect+track+gate stage before you add
   the second view.
